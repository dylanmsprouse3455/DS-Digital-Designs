Small Business Security Mistakes Most Owners Miss

Security problems often come from ordinary oversights: old plugins, unclear form handling, weak passwords, and domains nobody checks.

Reduce unnecessary moving parts

A simpler custom site can avoid plugin chains that need constant updates. Fewer dependencies mean fewer quiet failure points.

Protect forms and contact paths

Forms should avoid collecting information you do not need, clearly explain what happens next, and use reputable handling methods.

Keep ownership clear

Domains, hosting, analytics, and email accounts should be documented so the business is not locked out later.
